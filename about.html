About

UMACRAO is a non-profit, professional association focusing on issues of admissions, enrollment management, registration and other related issues of interest to members of the student services profession in higher education. UMACRAO members represent over 100 institutions of higher education from Iowa, Minnesota, North Dakota, South Dakota, Manitoba, Saskatchewan, and Northwestern Ontario.
