Committees

Complete a volunteer form if you are interested in joining one of our committees. UMACRAO committees offer an excellent opportunity to develop leadership skills and build connections. Appointment times normally last two years. Auditing Committee The auditing committee meets once a year at the annual conference to audit the association’s accounts, recommends accounting procedures, and reports back to the membership. Audit Committee Chair: Susan Battles, Concordia University – St. Paul Communications & Technology Committee The technology committee maintains and manages the UMACRAO listserv and website. This committee develops and uses technology to facilitate UMACRAO operations and member communication, including a quarterly newsletter and a special post-annual conference newsletter. Communication and Technology Chair: Elissa Thoman, University of Iowa Alissa Bigelow Kelly Freisleben Marissa Timmerman Thomas Reburn Corporate Relations Committee Works closely with the LAC and Program Committees, the vendor committee solicits vendors to participate in, present at, and host functions for the annual meeting. Corporate Liaison Co-Chair: Jessica Alberhasky, University of Iowa Corporate Liaison Co-Chair: Elissa Thoman, University of Iowa Executive Committee Manages the business and affairs of the association. President: Amber Cellotti, University of Minnesota Twin Cities President-Elect: Sharlene Allen, Minnesota State Secretary: Kate Lewis, Concordia University – St. Paul Treasurer: Erik Sparby, University of Minnesota Twin Cities Historian The historian/ archivist is responsible for maintaining historical information of the organization. Historian: Erik Sparby, University of Minnesota Twin Cities Local Arrangements Committee The local arrangements committee serves as the host for the annual conference and provides for all physical arrangements of the conference:. Local Arrangements Committee Co-Chair: Sara Sullivan, University of Iowa Local Arrangements Committee Co-Chair: Samantha Schneekloth, Mount Mercy University Membership Committee The membership committee discovers new members for the organization, catalogs institutional and individual membership information, publishes the new member brochure, and plans a welcome session at the annual conference. Membership Committee Chair: Sarah Jegela, Minnesota State University Moorhead Nominating Committee The nominations committee selects and presents nominees from the active membership for elective officers. Program Committee The program committee develops the program for the annual conference by presenting ideas and securing presenters. This committee serves as the representative to AACRAO on issues of affirmative action, and exercises leadership in committing the regional membership toward the advancement of equal education opportunities. This committee also evaluates committees for equitable representation. Committee Chair: Chance McWorthy; Program Committee Chair: Rick Pospichal, Winona State Site Selection Committee The UMACRAO site selection committee finds information about and evaluates sites for the annual meeting. Site Selection Committee Chair: Julie Fell, University of Iowa Special Projects Committee Publishes the annual enrollment report summary and the membership directory and supervises referred special projects of benefit to the association. Special Recognition Committee The special recognition committee makes nominations to the Executive Committee for the election of Honorary Life Member and Exemplary Service Awards.
